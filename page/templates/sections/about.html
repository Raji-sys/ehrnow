About

Dr. Mamman is an orthopaedic spine surgeon known for his hard work and determination. Despite his leadership responsibilities, he remains humble and focused on delivering the best care possible.

Passionate about his work, Dr. Mamman consistently completes tasks efficiently. He values fairness and treats everyone with respect, qualities that have earned him a reputation for honesty and integrity.

Over his decade-long career, Dr. Mamman has successfully performed thousands of complex surgeries. He currently serves as Chief Consultant Spine and Orthopaedic Surgeon, as well as Deputy Head of Spine at the National Orthopaedic Hospital, Kano.

And a trainer who is presently a part time lecturer in the department of surgery Bayero University Kano, and the head of purple spine team of National Orthopaedic Hospital Dala, Kano.
