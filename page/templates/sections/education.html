Education

Dr. Mamman attended his primary and secondary school education in Okene and Zaria respectively, completing his secondary education at Science Secondary School, Giwa, Kaduna State. He served as both house captain and senior prefect (Headboy).

He earned his MBBS in 2004 from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, before obtaining a fellowship from the West African College of Surgeons in Orthopaedic and Trauma between 2006 and 2013 at NOH Kano.

In 2012, his spine surgery training began at Ghurki Trust Teaching Hospital in Lahore, Pakistan, specializing in spine surgeries. This was followed by international training in countries such as Egypt, Turkey, Dubai, and South Africa.
